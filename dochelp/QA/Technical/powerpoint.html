How do I save images from the Select A Point Climatologies that can be used in PowerPoint?

To save these images, simply click on them with the right button of your mouse.

This will prompt you to save it as a gif image that you can then put into your PowerPoint presentation.

Note: Animations from the Data Library, including the Maproom, can be saved as gif images and inserted into PowerPoint as well.

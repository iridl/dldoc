Finding Data

I want to find data for stations at the same elevation. How do I do that?

Elevation is one of the categories under which data can searched.

For example, consider the NOAA/NCDC/DAILY/GLOBALSOD dataset:

/SOURCES/.NOAA/.NCDC/.DAILY/.GLOBALSOD/

Click on "Searches" to the right of the map. You will then be able to type in your elevation limits (meters) in the table near the top of the page.

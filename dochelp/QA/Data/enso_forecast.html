Finding Data

Where can I find ENSO forecasts?

You can find forecast sea surface temperatures (SSTs) in multiple locations on our website.

  • http://iri.columbia.edu/climate/forecast/sst/index.html
  • For the Pacific Ocean portion of the maps, the forecast comes from the NCEP Coupled Model. Other forecast information can be found from the NCEP Climate Prediction Center (CPC):

    http://www.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/products/analysis_monitoring/lanina/

  • A summary of forecasts from numerous institutions can also be found at the Austailian Bureau of Meteorology (BOM) website:

    http://www.bom.gov.au/climate/ahead/ENSO-summary.shtml

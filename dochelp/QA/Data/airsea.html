Finding Data

Where are air-sea solar short wave radiation and net heat flux data located in the Data Library?

We have climatologies for net short wave radiation and net heat flux into the ocean.

See the following datasets:

  • http://ingrid.ldgo.columbia.edu/SOURCES/.SOC/.GASC97/
  • /SOURCES/.OBERHUBER/
  • http://ingrid.ldgo.columbia.edu/SOURCES/.CAYAN/

believe all these are based on COADS data, but please be sure to look at the accompanying documentation (in blue menu bar on left side of page) to make sure that the data are what you need.

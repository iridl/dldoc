Finding Data

I am looking for information about hurricanes that passed along the southern coast of Mexico in 1971-1973. Where should I start?

In the IRI Data Library, we have gridded daily rainfall analyses from the Climate Prediction Center (CPC) for the United States/Mexico region. The archived data are at 1 x 1 degree resolution and extend from Jan 1, 1948 to Apr 30, 1999. This dataset can be found here:

http://iridl.ldeo.columbia.edu/SOURCES/.NOAA/.NCEP/.CPC/.REGIONAL/.US_Mexico/.daily/.gridded/.archive

p>

You may also want to look at the information on the following website of the U.S. National Hurricane Center (NHC):

http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/pastall.html

This site has a wealth of storm-specific information.

- Michael Bell
